About

Explore Travel Agency is a 501c3 nonprofit organization dedicated to the preservation of Columbiana’s unique heritage and sustainable tourism designed to contribute to the area’s economic growth.
The charity hosts the Joy of Festivals Uncorked events annually, collaborates with local businesses, and works in conjunction with the Columbiana Area Chamber of Commerce.
